Substantial Shareholder Disclosures

Under Part XV of the Securities and Futures Ordinance, investors crossing the 5% ownership threshold in any HKEX-listed company must file a disclosure. Circular tracks every filing — so you can see who owns what, and who is accumulating or selling down.
