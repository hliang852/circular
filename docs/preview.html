What companies do with capital
tells you more than what they say.

Circular tracks every repurchase mandate, monthly buyback filing, and AGM date for HKEX-listed companies — and surfaces the signals that matter to equity analysts.

🔄
Share Repurchases
Monthly Return filings give you volume, price range, and cumulative progress against the AGM-approved mandate. We surface the VWAP floor as a price support level.
📋
Mandate Tracking
Every mandate is capped at 10% of issued capital by Listing Rule 10.06. Circular shows headroom remaining, float-adjusted impact, and probability of renewal.
⚡
Equity Issuances
Placings, convertible bonds, and rights issues directly offset buybacks. We show gross buybacks alongside net capital return so you see the true picture.
🤝
DI Cross-reference
When a company buys back shares AND a substantial shareholder is simultaneously accumulating, both buyers are competing for the same shrinking float.
What the signals mean
● Strong
The company is buying back shares below its own 12-month VWAP, AND a substantial shareholder is accumulating via DI filing. Both buyers are active at current prices.
● Moderate
The company is buying below its own VWAP with a consistency score of 3+, but no concurrent DI accumulation is detected.
● Mandate Renewer
Company has an AGM within 90 days with >65% probability of mandate renewal. Uninterrupted programme support into the next fiscal year.
