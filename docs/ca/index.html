HKEX Corporate Actions Tracker

What companies do with capital
tells you more than what they say.

Circular tracks every repurchase mandate, monthly buyback filing, equity issuance, and AGM date for HKEX-listed companies — and surfaces the signals that matter to equity analysts.

🔄
Share Repurchases
Monthly Return filings give you volume, price range, and cumulative progress against the AGM-approved mandate. We surface the VWAP floor — where the company itself has been buying — as a price support level.
📋
Mandate Tracking
Every mandate is capped at 10% of issued capital by Listing Rule 10.06. Circular shows headroom remaining, float-adjusted impact, and a probability score for renewal at the next AGM — before the programme exhausts.
⚡
Equity Issuances
Placings, convertible bonds, and rights issues directly offset buybacks. We show gross buybacks alongside net capital return (buybacks minus issuances) so you see the true picture, not just the headline number.
🤝
DI Cross-reference
When a company buys back shares AND a substantial shareholder is simultaneously accumulating, both buyers are competing for the same shrinking float. That compound signal is surfaced on every stock page.
